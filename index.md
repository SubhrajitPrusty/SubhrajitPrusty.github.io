## About Me

Hello! I'm the Lead Backend Engineer at [Bolna](https://bolna.ai), building a **voice AI orchestration platform** in Python that handles tens of thousands of concurrent calls across Ecommerce, EdTech, HealthTech, BFSI, and Hospitality. My day-to-day is a mix of distributed systems work — hardening async pipelines against race conditions, taming WebSocket lifecycles, and architecting multi-cluster Kubernetes infrastructure for latency-sensitive SIP and WebSocket voice services.

Previously, I spent nearly five years at [Skit.ai](https://skit.ai) building LLM-powered voicebots in Go, designing bidirectional gRPC audio-streaming telephony stacks, and being the on-call engineer for systems that processed millions of calls for 40+ enterprise clients.

I'm a Linux enthusiast and an avid open-source supporter. I recently shipped [kubedump](https://github.com/SubhrajitPrusty/kubedump), a Go CLI that snapshots live Kubernetes resources into clean, Git-trackable manifests. I care a lot about observability, idempotent systems, and infrastructure you can actually reason about.

Outside of work, I enjoy gaming, optimizing my home server setup, and experimenting with new tech stacks.

You can explore my work on my [GitHub profile](https://github.com/SubhrajitPrusty).
